About

I am a second-year PhD student at Inria, France, in the Flowers team supervised by Pierre-Yves Oudeyer. My research focuses on Reinforcement Learning, open-ended learning, and curiosity-driven exploration, with the goal of building autonomous agents capable of learning without human supervision, solely through interaction with their environment.
